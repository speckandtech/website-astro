## MICME, the future of Microphonization

### Soraya Aliyari - Micme Microphonization

### Ivan Kayongo - Micme Microphonization

MICME is a new platform specifically created to manage the microphonization system in large scale events. Smartphones are connected to rooms sound system, so microphones and hostesses are not necessary. Downloading is not required üîù Users can reserve their questions with just one click on their smartphones; the server automatically lists the requests in a chronological order. Micme allows for a saving of up to 2/3 of the cost of the traditional microphonization systems üí∏
